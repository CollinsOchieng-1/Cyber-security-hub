Our Story

Our learning platform was born from the need to educate internet users on how to protect their data and assets from online threats. The world is moving towards a digital evolution and as users we need to know how to stay safe while in the digital community. At Cyber Security Hub, we offer online trainings to both individulal and organizations at affordable prices with certificates awarded upon completion of our courses.

We have a 24 hour customer service team ready to answer questions and solve issues related to our platform. We also chat room where our students can have a one on one with our teachers. We have our monthly blog where we discuss everything relates to Cyber Security and hold bi-annual webinars with stake holders in the technology field.

Our mission is to create a community of innovative, talented and creative people in our country's cyberspace. We do this by providing mentorship, training, financing and networking opportunities.

Our vision is to provide powerful and unique opportunities that will help Kenya become a prosperous nation within the cyberspace.
